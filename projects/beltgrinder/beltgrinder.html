DIY Beltgrinder

in this project we are making a simple tool which shall be used as a help for our future projects, lets START!

The first thing we are going to need is some sort of an electrical motor which is going to run this machine, the speed and the power of the motor you choose is going to limit the size of the grider youre making, soo choose well, the bigger the motor the stronger the grinder.

Next step is making the stand for the whole thing, the part thats going to fixate the motor and both rollers together, now ive used some scrap metal i found around my garage, but you can buy yours its not expensive. Ive been using a welder, but you can also asemble the whole thing with screws, i just find welding to be the most efficient way as it reduces the overall weight and increases the resistance to vibrations, and trust me there will be lots of them.

Now we need some wheels/rollers which are going to move our belt sandpaper, i bought these off ebay cheap.

Next we need is a fixed table thats going to support our workpiece , i made mine out of some flat piece of metal i had laying around my garage

And the whole thing is done

This is a machine i will be using in the next guide on how to make some homemade blades and some cool knifes
