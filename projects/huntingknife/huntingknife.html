DIY Hunting knife

Todays project is a simple hunting knife, you can also make a similar kitchen knife the same way, just use a thinner steel.lets start!

The first thing we are going to need is a material which we are goning to use for this knife, so were going ot need some wood for the handle, some steel, and some pivits for binding thsoe two together.

When choosing steel, make sure you think about which legure youre going to use, as different precentage of carbon affects edge maintnance, and overall flexibility of the blade.

for wood, use whichever you like the most, as different types of wood have different patterns. im using nutwood as i find its sturdiness and its pattern the most suitable for my taste.

Pivits are mostly made out of brass, so just use some brass vire thats cut to a proper length.

Now lets get to the fun part! take a piece of paper and drawout the shape of a knife you whish to make! i made mine like this.

Now we need to cutout the shape in steel, were going to use teh beltgrinder we made in the last guide.

Make a nice bevel.

Now harden the darn thing by shoving it in to teh fordge till it glows white. after that quickly quench it into some water/oil, oil is better as its a bit slower cooling process.

Harden the blade on 300 degrees celcius, and shove it in oil to cool it down quickly.

its time to attach teh handle.

And make it nicely shaped and confy to the hand.

And for a final step polish the blade and sharpen teh edge till it can cut paper without problem

And the whole thing is done

This is well made DIY knife which can be used for anything, just watchout not to cutyourself.
